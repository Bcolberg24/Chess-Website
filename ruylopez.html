The Ruy Lopez

The Ruy Lopez (Spanish Opening) is one of the most famous and deeply explored openings in chess. It was named after Spanish priest Ruy Lopez de Segura who studied the opening in his book on chess written in 1561. The opening starts after 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bb5 and branches off into hundreds of lines.

Below is a list of some of the most important lines in the Ruy Lopez
