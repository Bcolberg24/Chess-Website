The Vienna Game

The Vienna Game is a somewhat uncommon opening for white and starts with 1. e4 e5 2. Nc3, yet is still perfectly sound and can be very tricky. The Vienna Gambit in particular make a normal looking move for black have immediately losing consequences for the black pieces. Additionally, the main line of this opening is rare to stumble across without having studied it previously and therefore the Vienna makes for a very tricky and fun opening for white.
