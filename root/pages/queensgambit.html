The Queen's Gambit

The Queen's Gambit starts with 1. d4 d5 2. c4 and is one of the most well-known and reputable openings starting with 1. d4. This is a great opening for every skill level and typically leads to a more strategic and maneuvering game.

Below is a list of some of the most important lines in the Queen's Gambit

  • Queen's Gambit Accepted
  • Queen's Gambit Declined
  • Slav Defense
  • Albin Counter Gambit
