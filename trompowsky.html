The Trompowsky

The Trompowsky is a unique and tricky opening for white after 1. d4 Nf6 2. Bg5. Playing the Trompowsky can immediately throw off your opponent and get you in comfortable territory. However, the opening is extremely sharp and can have many different variations that require some memorization.
