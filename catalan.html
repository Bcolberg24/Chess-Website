The Catalan

The Catalan is essentially a variation of the Queen's Gambit after 1. d4 d5 2. c4 except in this variation white forfeits the common idea of utilizing the bishop on f1 to defend the c4 pawn and instead chooses to fianchetto their bishop on g2. This is a highly theoretical and positional opening and is therefore in a separate category to the Queen's Gambit.
