Chess Openings Guide

All chess openings can be categorized by some basic criteria. Firstly, are you playing with the white pieces or the black pieces. Generally, white has only a few options to start the game, most commonly e4 and d4. From there, it is important to continue to gain control of the center by playing the other center pawn forward two spaces. However, black will typically prevent that by playing certain defenses.
