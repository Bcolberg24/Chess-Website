The Scotch Game

The Scotch is a nice weapon for beginners to play in place of the Ruy Lopez. It begins with 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. d4 and it allows white to gain space in the center quickly and avoids many highly theoretical lines in the Spanish. Because the Scotch is not analyzed nearly as much as openings like the Ruy Lopez, becoming a master in this opening can put your opponent at a disadvantage quickly.
